Mastercard Internship Process (Through PICT) – Detailed Overview

1. *Initial Screening (Google Form)*

The process begins with a Google Form shared with students.

*Eligibility Criteria:*
- Candidates must have a **CGPA greater than 8.25**

Students meeting this criterion are shortlisted for the Online Assessment round.

--------------------------------------------------

2. *Online Assessment (OA)*

- *Duration:* 1 hour  
- *Format:* 2 coding questions  

*Topics Covered:*
- Dynamic Programming (DP)  
- Graph-based problems  

*Difficulty Level:*
- Comparable to *medium-level problems on LeetCode*

--------------------------------------------------

3. *Shortlisting Statistics*

- Approximately **700 students** were eligible for the OA  
- Only **80 students from PICT** were shortlisted for the next round  

--------------------------------------------------

4. *Hackathon Round – “Code for Change”*

The shortlisted candidates participate in a hackathon.

*Total Participants:* ~200 students  

- 80 from PICT  
- 120 from other colleges (COEP, AIT Pune, VIT, Cummins)  

--------------------------------------------------

5. *Team Structure*

Each team consists of:

- **8 students** (from different colleges)  
- **1 mentor** (Mastercard employee)  

*Mentors may include:*
- Principal Engineer  
- Senior Engineer  
- Director  
- SDE-3  

--------------------------------------------------

6. *Hackathon Schedule*

- *Start Time:* 7:00 AM  
- *Coding Ends:* 5:00 PM  
  - No coding is allowed after this time  
- The *presentation (PPT)* must be completed before 5:00 PM  
- A PPT template is provided by Mastercard  

--------------------------------------------------

7. *Presentation Round*

- Begins after 5:00 PM  
- Concludes around 9:30 PM  

*Format:*
- 5 minutes for presentation  
- 2 minutes for Q&A  

--------------------------------------------------

8. *Facilities Provided*

Participants are provided with:

- Breakfast  
- Lunch  
- Dinner  

--------------------------------------------------

9. *Internship Selection Criteria*

Selection is *not based on hackathon rankings*.

Evaluation is based on *individual performance*:

- Technical knowledge  
- Problem-solving ability  
- Teamwork and collaboration  
- Communication and presentation skills  
- Overall contribution during the hackathon  

--------------------------------------------------

10. *Final Selection Statistics*

- Out of **80 students from PICT**, approximately **35 students** were selected  

--------------------------------------------------

11. *Important Strategy*

If selected for the hackathon:

- Focus on *demonstrating your individual skills*  
- Actively contribute within your team  
- Communicate effectively with your mentor  

*Note:*
- The mentor has a **significant influence (~85%)** on the final selection  

--------------------------------------------------

12. *Final Note*

The above details are based on the *PICT 2027 batch recruitment cycle*.

For future batches, these parameters may change depending on:

- Company requirements  
- Number of applicants  
- Hiring strategy
